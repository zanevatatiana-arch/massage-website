About Me

My name is Galina and I am a qualified massage therapist with 10 years of experience. Throughout my career I have supported a wide range of clients, helping them reduce muscle tension, ease chronic discomfort and improve their overall wellbeing.

I hold a professional certification in massage therapy and continue to develop my skills through ongoing training and modern therapeutic techniques. My approach is calm, personalised and focused on creating a relaxing environment where clients can fully unwind.

Whether you are looking for stress relief, deep muscle relaxation or targeted deep tissue treatment, every session is tailored to meet your individual needs.

The studio is based in Wokingham and offers a peaceful, private setting. Treatments are available by appointment only.
